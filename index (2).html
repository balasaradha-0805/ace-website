About ACE

The Association of Computing Engineers (ACE) is the official club of the School of Computing at SASTRA Deemed University. Established with the aim of promoting excellence in computing education and research, ACE organizes a wide range of events, hackathons, webinars and workshops throughout the academic year. Driven by the principles of innovation, excellence, collaboration, education, and community, ACE provides students with opportunities to enhance their technical skills, explore emerging technologies, and connect with industry professionals. At ACE, our mission is to create an exciting and collaborative environment within the School of Computing. We want to help students thrive in the world of computing through events, hackathons, webinars, and more.

ACE welcomes all students with a passion for computing to join us in our journey of learning, innovation, and growth. Come be a part of our vibrant community and unleash your potential in the exciting world of computing

Mission of ACE

At ACE, our mission is to create an exciting and collaborative environment within the School of Computing. We want to help students thrive in the world of computing through events, hackathons, webinars, and more. Here's what we're all about:

1. Inspiring Innovation: We want to spark creativity and innovation in our members. We provide a space for students to explore new technologies, try out fresh ideas, and solve realworld problems.

2. Supporting Collaboration: Collaboration is key in the fast-paced world of computing. ACE is a place where students can connect with peers, faculty, and industry professionals. We believe in teamwork and sharing ideas.

3. Encouraging Excellence: We're all about promoting excellence in computing education and research. Our events, workshops, and seminars are designed to help members build their skills, knowledge, and careers.

4. Research Advancement: ACE encourages members to engage in research and scholarly activities, contributing to the advancement of computing knowledge through publications and collaborations by connecting you with Faculties and Research scholars of SoC.
